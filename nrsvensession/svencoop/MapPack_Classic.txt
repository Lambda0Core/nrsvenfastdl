To run these maps on your server, add the following text into
your SvenCoop\mapcycle.txt file:

gmantowers
durga
infiltrate
blackops
assaultmesa2
garghnt3
jailbreak
megamonstermassacre
nightmare-c1
svencooprpg
gruntwar
opr1a1
gut_reaction
sc_spaceviking1
powernode1
phobia
bunker
grunts
horde
war
undeath
exhale1platinum
helebat3

following text into your SvenCoop\mapvote.cfg file:

addvotemap gmantowers
addvotemap durga
addvotemap infiltrate
addvotemap blackops
addvotemap assaultmesa2
