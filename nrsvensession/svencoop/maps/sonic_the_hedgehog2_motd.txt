

Welcome to Sonic The Hedgehog2


Story: You have to go through all 6 zones 
to get to Dr.Eggman's Winged Fortress. Along 
the way there are 7 Hidden Chaos Emeralds that 
you have to find to unlock Hyper Sonic mode.
Once you destroy the power core you can teleport
to the final room where the master emerald is.
